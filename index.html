Welcome to Craftscade Earth

Craftscade Earth is a unique Minecraft Java Edition server that places you in a massive, Earth-like map where towns, nations, and alliances shape the course of history.

Why Play on Craftscade Earth?

  • 🌍 A custom Earth map at 1:500 scale — claim land and settle real-world regions
  • 🏙️ Create and govern your own towns and nations
  • 🛒 Player-run economy — no admin shops, real trade with gold currency
  • 🤝 Tight-knit and active community
  • 📜 Rich roleplay and politics, shaped by players
  • ⚙️ Vanilla-feel with performance optimizations and enhancements

What to Expect

Craftscade Earth is easy to join but hard to master. Whether you’re a builder, a strategist, or a social butterfly, there’s a place for you to make an impact.

Honest Server Notes

  • 🌐 Server is hosted in Europe — ping may vary by region
